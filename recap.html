BRAIL

Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory or also known as BRAIL is a robot team from Batam State Polytechnic, There is many projects that do on BRAIL. It is a place for students to learn about robotics and artificial intelligence, level up their skills by doing Project Based Learning (PBL).

Barelang 63

Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory or also known as BRAIL is a robot team from Batam State Polytechnic, Barelang 63 is one of the brail branches that focuses on developing wheeled soccer robots. Barelang 63 has competed in soccer robotics especially in middle size league, since 2021 and managed to make many achivements.
